Market Reality

What We Solve

Morocco’s real estate market has a persistent information gap.

Premium developments are often marketed professionally, but much of the mid-market remains under-described, inconsistently positioned, and difficult for buyers to evaluate, especially for those navigating the process from abroad.

We close that gap by bringing structure, verification, and clear presentation to opportunities that are often overlooked or misunderstood, allowing both international and local buyers to make informed decisions.

Profiles

Who We Help

We work with buyers and developers who need more than access to listings—they need clarity, structure, and the ability to make the right decision in a market that is not always transparent.

Moroccan Diaspora (MRE)

You are working within a limited timeframe when you come to Morocco. You may rely on family to visit properties and provide feedback—but they are not evaluating the market professionally.

We help you replace uncertainty with structure so you can identify the right opportunities and move forward with confidence.

International Buyers

You are exploring Morocco as an investment or lifestyle market, but the process is unfamiliar. Information is scattered, and it is difficult to evaluate opportunities from a distance.

We provide the context, verification, and guidance needed to make a well-informed decision.

Local Investors

You already know the market, but access to the right opportunities and clear positioning remains limited.

We help you identify better-positioned projects and evaluate value more precisely.

Real Estate Developers

You have a strong project, but reaching the right audience—especially international buyers—requires more than standard marketing.

We help position your project clearly and connect it with the right buyers.

Elaf Sky View,
Centre Ville Kénitra.

A current Rahim International mandate: 35 high-spec apartments in central Kénitra for buyers who want location, build quality, and stronger value than Rabat pricing typically allows.

Kénitra · City Centre
Elaf Sky View
Apartments Centre Ville Energy Ready High-Spec Finishes Daam Sakani Eligible Active Mandate

Elaf Sky View is a 35-unit residential development in Centre Ville Kénitra, close to the TGV station, motorway access, public gardens, and everyday amenities.

The project stands out because the specification is unusually strong for the price point: double-insulated windows and glass doors, air-conditioning pre-installation, fully equipped kitchens, Turkish-imported doors with triple deadbolt systems, elevated interior finishes, and assigned underground parking included in the title.

Each apartment also includes private rooftop allocation for future solar panels, connected to the unit and the electric input for its underground parking stall.
