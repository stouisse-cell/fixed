The Address

The heart of Kénitra.

Elaf Sky View sits on Avenue Youssef Ben Tachfine, approximately 300 metres from its intersection with Rue Mohammed V — the main artery that runs through Kénitra and every major city in Morocco. It is one of those addresses that needs no explanation to anyone who knows the country.

Step outside and within the same few streets you have everything you need for daily life — fresh produce, a pharmacy, a clinic, your bank, a gym, cafés, restaurants, schools, and taxis running constantly. The Corniche Sebou boardwalk, the Jardin de la Province, and the city's main commercial avenues are minutes away on foot.

This kind of central address is increasingly rare in Kénitra. The city is growing — but it is growing outward. What is already here stays here.

70KMAD Grant

Daam Sakani

A government grant.
On a centre ville address.

The Daam Sakani programme is the Moroccan government's direct housing grant for first-time buyers. Selected entry units at Elaf Sky View qualify. Most Daam Sakani purchases in Kénitra happen in newer developments on the city's outskirts. Applying the same grant to a centre ville address on Avenue Youssef Ben Tachfine is a different proposition — the same grant, a fundamentally different location.

Your Investment Consultation will confirm eligibility, which units qualify, and what the grant means for your specific purchase numbers.
